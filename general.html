The Insights

The following graphs provide data on general drivers in the United States.

The pie graph displays the percentage of males and females that drive from 1994-2023. The amount of drivers is almost split up exactly evenly! Is it a surprise that there are almost an equal amount of male and female drivers?

This line graph displays the drivers per year from 1994-2023. The relationship between the amount of drivers and years is linear. As the years increase, the amount of drivers also increase. There is a slight plateau from 2011 to 2013. It can be infered that this may be due an increase in economic and/or population growth over the years.
